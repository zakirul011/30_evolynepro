A Few Words About us

We’re an all-American company operating near the beautiful city of Dallas, TX. We’re indebted to this beautiful city and its people. This is why we’ve decided to build a brand completely run by Texans for the outstanding people of Texas and the greater American public.

Our full synthetic oil is a synthesis of our highest quality synthetic stocks and superior quality additives, creating the ultimate oil that your engine craves.

Superior Quality Additives

evolynepro special diesel blend contains specific additives and higher viscosity lubricants to ensure your engine runs properly. With a higher zinc concentration, our diesel oil helps prevent corrosion.
